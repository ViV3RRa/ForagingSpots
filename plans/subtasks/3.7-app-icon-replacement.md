## Task

1. **Point every icon reference at the new set** (prefer referencing `public/app-icon/…` paths
   directly and deleting the old root-level files, rather than copying/renaming — one canonical
   set):
   - `index.html`: favicon → `/app-icon/icon-40.png` (or a larger size; correct the `type`
     attribute), apple-touch-icon 180 → `icon-180.png`, 152 → `icon-152.png`,
     120 → `icon-120.png`.
   - `vite.config.ts` manifest icons: 192 → `app-icon/icon-192.png`,
     512 → `app-icon/icon-512.png`.
   - `includeAssets`: replace the phantom entries with the icon files actually referenced from
     `index.html` (manifest icons are picked up by the plugin automatically).
2. **Maskable icon:** the exported PNGs are full-bleed with the monogram centered. Verify the
   "S" + rings sit inside the maskable safe zone (inner ~80% circle — check at
   https://maskable.app or by inspecting proportions from the master). If it clears, reuse
   `icon-512.png` for the `purpose: 'maskable'` entry (keep separate `any` and `maskable`
   entries rather than the deprecated combined `'any maskable'`). If it does not clear,
   generate `public/app-icon/icon-512-maskable.png` from `icon-1024.png` by scaling the artwork
   to ~80% on a terracotta `#B5502F` canvas (macOS `sips` can't composite — use a small
   node/sharp or canvas script, committed under `scripts/`).
3. **Delete superseded assets:** the six old `public/` icon files listed above, once nothing
   references them (`grep -r` for each filename across the repo first — `offline.html` and the
   PocketBase templates are easy to miss).
4. **Rebuild** and confirm the Workbox precache includes the new icons and drops the old ones.
