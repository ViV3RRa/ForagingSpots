## Findings

   `pocketbase/pb_data` is in v0.23+ format (contains `_superusers`, v0.23 migrations, and a
   Nov 2025 system migration). `./pocketbase serve` limps along with a
   "Settings load error … no such column: key" warning, the admin CLI is broken
   ("Migrations are not initialized"), and public user creation 400s. QA therefore ran a newer
   binary (0.37.5) against a *copy* of pb_data. **Fix:** upgrade the committed binary to ≥ the
   version that produced pb_data.
